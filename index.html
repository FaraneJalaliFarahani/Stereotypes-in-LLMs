Instructions — please read before starting

You will be shown an antonym pair (e.g. poor ↔ rich) and a word (e.g. students). Your task is to rate where that word falls on the scale between the two poles.

poor  ←  1 · 2 · 3 · 4 · 5  →  rich
Are "students" rather poor, rather rich, or neither?
Pick 1 (clearly poor-side), 3 (neither), or 5 (clearly rich-side) — or anything in between.
If the scale does not make sense for the word (e.g. rating a table on poor↔rich), mark it Not applicable.

Neither (3) vs. Not applicable: Use 3 — neither when the word sits in the middle of the scale. Use Not applicable when the scale simply does not apply to the word at all (e.g. a physical object on a personality scale).

For a small number of word pairs you will also be asked to suggest one word that clearly belongs at each end of the scale — this can be any word, not only a social group.
